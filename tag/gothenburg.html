As a master student mayoring in Machine Learning I've tried to research what companies and institutions in Gothenburg that does Machine Learning or Big data-related stuff. I was looking for future work place and trying to understand how the Machine Learning community in GBG worked.

I thought I would share what I've found. This list is probably not complete and I would love to add information about your company or research group as well as update or change the information given below.
