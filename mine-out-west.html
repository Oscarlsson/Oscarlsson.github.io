Mine Out West at Hack Out West 2016

  • by Oscar Carlsson
  • on Thu 11 August 2016

Category: hacking

As part of a hackathon called Hack Out West we built the Way Out West 2016 festival into minecraft.

We generated the landscape from maps, built the stage areas and implemented so that the artists/bands spawned at the right stage when they were going on stage. All great fun. Watch the video below to check it out :)
